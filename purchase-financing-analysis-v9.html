Changelog

v9 — Regime Engine & Probability-Weighted Analysis

  • Regime-Based MC: Replaces single-vol GBM with discrete recession/expansion regimes. Quarter-by-quarter simulation with stochastic regime transitions, separate vol and return parameters for each state. Models recession as short, sharp episodes (avg 12mo) — not sustained multi-year decline.
  • Portfolio Return → 11%: Reflects a diversified multi-strategy blend (e.g. 10%/9%/14% across three allocations). Expansion returns use this as the mean; recession returns are modeled separately.
  • Expansion Vol → 11%: Historical non-recession S&P vol. Down from 14% blended all-regime vol. Recession vol is modeled separately at 22%.
  • Probability-Weighted Stress Test: Each scenario carries an explicit probability weight (15% recession, 20% stagnation, 45% base, 20% bull). Weighted expected wealth shows mortgage advantage across the full probability distribution.
  • Realistic Bear Case: "Recession + Recovery" replaces the old -3%/yr for 7 years. Models 12mo recession at -18%/yr within the hold, with remainder at normalized 11% return. Blended annual return is ~+5%, not -3%.
  • After-Tax Spread: New metric in main table and stress panel showing portfolio return × (1−tax) minus effective borrowing cost. Makes the core leverage thesis explicit.
  • Regime MC Parameters: New slider section: recession probability/year, avg duration, recession/expansion returns and vol, rate drop during recession.

v7 — Prior Version

  • Independent Down Payments: ARM, fixed, and interest-only strategies now use separate down-payment inputs.
  • MID Realization Factor: Mortgage-interest deduction benefits are haircut for real-world itemization/SALT effects.
  • Binary-Search Breakevens: Breakeven investment returns are now solved with a proper binary search across holding periods.
  • Decision Summary: Expected-value, risk-adjusted, and sleep-well winners are displayed separately.
  • Updated Defaults: Portfolio tax drag, volatility, correlation, Monte Carlo runs, and down-payment assumptions updated.

v5 — Modeling & UX Overhaul

  • Dynamic Annual Drags: Property tax & maintenance now deducted from the portfolio each year rather than as a lump sum at exit. Reduces compounding basis realistically.
  • Full ARM Schedule: Annual resets after year 7 with periodic (2%/yr) and lifetime (5%) caps, plus index + margin inputs. Feeds through MC volatility engine.
  • Additional Strategy: Interest-only ARM option added for jumbo buyers.
  • Richer MC Output: Canvas histogram of wealth distributions, probability of leverage beating cash by >$X thresholds.
  • Assumption Transparency: Expandable Model Notes panel explaining every rate, limitation, and assumption.
  • Export: Share URL (base64 state) and CSV export of MC distributions.
  • Input validation: Dollar fields reject negative values.
  • Tooltips: Key metrics have hover explanations.
  • Mobile polish: Horizontal scroll on wide tables with visible scrollbar indicators.
  • Performance: Memoized scenario calculations to avoid redundant re-runs in stress/sensitivity.
