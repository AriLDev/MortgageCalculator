Changelog

v8 — Analytical Depth & MC Engine Upgrade

  • Stochastic ARM Index (#1): MC engine now simulates correlated SOFR paths. Rate volatility and rate-equity correlation are configurable. ARM resets use per-year stochastic index rather than a fixed value.
  • Decision Summary Fix (#2): MC median is now primary "Best Expected Outcome" (captures geometric compounding). Deterministic case correctly labeled as optimistic.
  • Negative Cashflow (#3): Portfolio calculation handles negative monthly contributions robustly — portfolio floors at zero, contributions and withdrawals tracked separately.
  • Computed MID Realization (#4): Auto-computes effective MID benefit vs. standard deduction + other itemized deductions. Toggle to override with manual slider.
  • Variable Tax Drag (#7): Portfolio tax drag varies with annual returns — higher in up years (distributions), near-zero in down years (tax-loss harvesting).
  • Assessment Lag (#8): Property tax based on 2-year-lagged assessed value, matching Colorado reassessment schedule.
  • Wealth Difference Distribution (#11): New histogram showing per-path (best leverage − cash) with zero line, median annotation, and P(loss).
  • Wealth Trajectory (#12): Year-by-year net wealth chart showing when leverage crosses over cash and where maximum regret occurs.
  • Scaled MC Thresholds (#15): Probability thresholds now scale with purchase price (1.5%, 3%, 7.5%) instead of fixed $50K/$100K/$250K.
  • Breakeven Color-Coding (#19): Green/red indicators show whether current return assumption exceeds each breakeven threshold, with margin displayed.
  • Custom Stress Scenario (#20): User-configurable market return and home appreciation with inline sliders in the stress test section.
  • Mobile Abbreviations (#21): Strategy names abbreviate on narrow viewports to prevent table overflow.

v7 — Financing Decision Model Updates

  • Independent Down Payments: ARM, fixed, and interest-only strategies now use separate down-payment inputs.
  • MID Realization Factor: Mortgage-interest deduction benefits are haircut for real-world itemization/SALT effects.
  • Binary-Search Breakevens: Breakeven investment returns are now solved with a proper binary search across holding periods.
  • Decision Summary: Expected-value, risk-adjusted, and sleep-well winners are displayed separately.
  • Updated Defaults: Portfolio tax drag, volatility, correlation, Monte Carlo runs, and down-payment assumptions updated.

v5 — Modeling & UX Overhaul

  • Dynamic Annual Drags: Property tax & maintenance now deducted from the portfolio each year rather than as a lump sum at exit. Reduces compounding basis realistically.
  • Full ARM Schedule: Annual resets after year 7 with periodic (2%/yr) and lifetime (5%) caps, plus index + margin inputs. Feeds through MC volatility engine.
  • Additional Strategy: Interest-only ARM option added for jumbo buyers.
  • Richer MC Output: Canvas histogram of wealth distributions, probability of leverage beating cash by >$X thresholds.
  • Assumption Transparency: Expandable Model Notes panel explaining every rate, limitation, and assumption.
  • Export: Share URL (base64 state) and CSV export of MC distributions.
  • Input validation: Dollar fields reject negative values.
  • Tooltips: Key metrics have hover explanations.
  • Mobile polish: Horizontal scroll on wide tables with visible scrollbar indicators.
  • Performance: Memoized scenario calculations to avoid redundant re-runs in stress/sensitivity.
